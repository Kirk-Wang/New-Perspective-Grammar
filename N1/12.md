# 口语

### 商场购物
* Can I help you ?
  * Ca`n I` -> \nai\
  * hel`p y`ou -> \pju:\

**连读规则：一辅一元，前辅后元**
* Clean it up
  * clea`n i`t
  * i`t u`p
* put it on
  * pu`t i`t
  * i`t o`n
* turn it up
  * tur`n i`t
  * i`t u`p
* isn't it
  * `'t` 省掉
  * is`n i`t
* it under it
  * i`t u`nder
  * unde`r i`t
* Could you help me?
  * coul`d y`ou
