### 一般疑问句(be 动词类别-->词性维度)
* Are you ...?
* Is she...?
* Am I...?
* Were you...?
* Was she...?
* `+ 非动词`

**EG.**
* Are you `at home`? prep. 介词词组
* Is she `a teacher`? n. 名词
* Am I `right`? adj. 形容词
* Were you `there`? adv.副词
* Was she `your friend`? pron. 代词词组
* be 动词类别提问 -->> 后面只能加非动词词性

**经常的状态**
* Are you...?
* Is she...?
* Am I...?

**过去(经常)的状态**
* Were you...?
* Was she...?

**练习**
* 你是老师吗？
  * Are you a teacher?
* 你昨天在家吗？
  * Were you at home yesterday?
* 她上周缺课了吗？
  * Was she absent last week?
* 他刚刚在这吗？
  * Was he here just now?
* 她在北京吗？
  * Is she in Beijing?

### 一般疑问句(is/am/are/was/were作为助动词方式提问)
* Are you ...?
* Is she...?
* Am I...?
* Were you...?
* Was she...?
* `+ doing...`

**EG**
* Are you `studying English`?
  * 你正在学英语吗？
* Is she `cooking`?
  * 她正在做饭吗？
* Is he `having a bath`?
  * 他正在洗澡吗？
* Were you `doing your homework`?
  * 你正在做作业吗？(过去的某一点)
* Was she `waiting for him`?
  * 她正在等她吗？(过去的某一点)

**此时此刻正在**(现在进行时)
* Are you doing ...?
* Is she doing...?
* Am I doing...?

**那时那刻正在**(过去进行时))
* Were you doing...?
* Was she doing...?

**练习**
* 你正在学法语吗？
  * Are you studying French?
* 你昨晚8点正在约会吗？
  * Were you having a date at 8:00 last night.
* 她正在度假吗？
  * 现在进行
  * Is she having a holiday?
* 我到的时候他正在读书吗？
  * 过去进行
  * Was he reading when I arrived?

### Will & Shall(作为助动词提问)
* 将来的时态
* Will you...?
* Will she...?
* Shall I...?
  * Shall 只能用于第一人称，Will 都可以
* Shall we...?
* + do(动词原形)
* Will you `study English`?
* Will she `cook`?
* Will he `have a bath`?
* Shall we `have a holiday` next month?
* Shall I `wait for him`?

**将要**
* Will you...?
* Will she...?
* Shall I...?
* Shall we...?

**练习**
* 你要学法语吗？
  * Will you study English?
* 他们晚上要约会吗？
  * Will they have a date tonight?
* 我们去度假好吗？
  * Shall we have a holiday?
* 你们出国读书吗？
  * Will you study abroad?

### Have/Has/Had(助动词)
* Have you...?
* Has she...?
* Had you...?
* Had she...?
* + done(动词过去分词)
* Have you `finished` your study?
* Has she `cooked` the meal?
* Has he `had` a bath?
* Had you `done` your homework before 2:00?
* Had she `opened` the door before I arrived?

**截止现在已经完成**
* Have you...?
* Has she...?

**截止过去已经完成**
* Had you...?
* Had she...?

**练习** （ll7&ll9）
* 你学过法语吗？
  * Have you studied French?
* 他们约会过吗？
  * Have they had a date?
* 你们度假前考试了吗？
  * Had you taken the exam before holiday?
* 你到之前火车走了吗？
  * Had the trian left before you arrived?

### Do/Does/Did(助动词)
* Do you...?
* Does she...?
* Did you\she...?
* + do(动词原形)
* Do you `study English`?
  * 你经常学英语吗？
* Does she `cook`?
  * 她经常做饭吗？
* Did she `have a bath`?
  * 她有洗澡吗？（过去的某个时间点）
* Does she `do her homework`?
  * 她经常做家庭作业吗？
* Did you `wait for him`?
  * 你有没有等他？（过去的某个时间点）

**习惯的动作**
* Do you...?
* Does she...?

**过去（习惯的）动作**
* Did you\she...?

**练习：**
* 你经常游泳吗？
 * Do you (usually) swim?
  * usually 可加可不加
  * 这个时态就表示经常的动作
* 他经常约会吗？
 * Does he (usually) have a date?
* 你们去年度假了吗？
 * Did you have a holiday last year?
* 他今早赶上火车了吗？
 * Did he catch the train this morning?

### Can/Must/May/Could
* 情态动词的提问方式……
* Can you...?
* Can she...?
* Must I...?
* May I...?
* Could you...?
* +do(动词原形)
* Can you `cook`?
  * 你会做饭吗？
* Can she `swim`?
  * 她会游泳吗？
* May I `have a seat`?
  * 我可以坐在这里吗？
* Must I `do that`?
  * 我必须要做这件事吗？
* Could you `show` me another one?
  * 您可以在给我拿一件吗？

**能够&可以**
* Can you...?
* Can she...?

**必须**
* Must I...?

**能够&可以**
* May I...?
* Could you...?
  * Could 是 can 的过去式
  * 这里是表示委婉的说法

**练习**
* 我可以请你看电影吗？
  * May I invite you to watch a movie?
* 你可以帮我一个忙吗？
  * Could you give me a hand?
* 你可以自己穿衣服吗？
  * Can you put on your coat by yourself?
* 我必须站在这吗？
  * Must I stand here?
