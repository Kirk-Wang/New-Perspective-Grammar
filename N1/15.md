# 时态在口语中

### 1. 一般现在

A. 经常的状态：主语 + is\am\are

B. 习惯的动作：主语 + v.(动词)

C. 真理。主语 + is\am\are，主语 + v.(动词)

**口语句型**

A.
- Do you often...?
- Yes, I do.
- No, I don't.

B.
- Does he often...?
- Yes, he does.
- No, he doesn't.

C.
- What do you usually do ...?(in the morning)
- I usually swim\go shopping....
