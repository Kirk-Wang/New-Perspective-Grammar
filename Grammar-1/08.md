# 08.简单句的核心变化 谓语情态【真题演练】

```
Upcoming reforms might bring the price 
to a more reasonable level.
(2017, Reading Comprehension, Part A Text 1)
Upcoming：即将到来的
reform：改革
reasonable level：合理的水平
`might bring`（谓语动词）: might 表示推测可能怎样
即将到来的改革可能会把价格带到一个合理的水平

Employers wouldn't mind a little fewer bubbles in 
the job market.
(2014, Reading Comprehension, Part A Text 3)
Employers：雇主
bubbles：泡沫
`wouldn't mind`: 谓语动词
would:将要
wouldn't:将不会
雇主们将不会介意有一些更少的泡沫在人才市场上。

...but we `cannot escape` the context of our unique life experience.
(2012, Reading Comprehension, Part A Text 3)
context：上下文当中
unique：独特的
life experience:生活经历，生活的经验
cannot escape: 谓语动词部分,不能逃离
我们不能逃离背景，我们独特的生活经历的背景

...we must press forward on deeper atmospheric and oceanic research.
(2005, Reading Comprehension, Part A Text 2)
atmospheric: 大气的
oceanic：海洋的
research：研究
press：按压
forward：向前
`must press`
我必须向前推进更深层次的研究，一个是大气的，一个是海洋的

Your outline `should` smoothly `conduct` you from one point to the next...
(2008, Reading Comprehension, Part B)

outline:提纲，大纲
should conduct: 应该做某事
smoothly: 顺利地，平滑地

你的大纲应该非常顺利地引导你从一个观点到另一个进行过度……
```
