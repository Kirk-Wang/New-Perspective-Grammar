# 10.简单句的核心变化 谓语语态【真题演练】

```
By the third generation, the original language is
lost in the majority of immigrant families.
(2006, Reading Comprehension, Part A Text 1)
majority：大多数
immigrant families：移民的家庭👨‍👩‍👧‍👦
is lost: 谓语动词，一般现在时的被动语态
截止到第三代，移民家庭中的大多数母语就被弄丢了
```

```
A great deal of attention is being paid today to the
so-called digital divide...
(2001, Reading Comprehension, Part A Text 2)
A great deal of: 许多大量的...+不可数名词
attention:注意力，不可数
so-called：所谓的，表示不是真的
digital divide：数字鸿沟
is being paid：现在进行时的被动，现在正在被付出
人们正在被注意数字鸿沟
```

```
The sentences within each paragraph `should be related` to a single topic.
(2008, Reading Comprehension, Part B)
谓语动词：should be related
情态动词的被动，表示应该被联系到
在一段话当中的多个句子应该被联系到一个单一的主题
```
